Only fill in the boxes for the highest level of finish for each character! You don't count the individual steps that make up the character -- only the most complete of those categories.

For example

If you have a piece featuring 1 full-body character who is fully shaded, you do not fill in 1 box for lines, color, and shading -- only fill in 1 shaded under the Full-body section!

The option to select multiple levels of finish is meant for pieces where you may have varying degrees of finish such as doodle dumps.

For a character to count as appearing in a piece for rewards, they need to have some sort of identifiable features!

For example

A character will not count towards rewards if they are shown in one of these ways (or similarly):

only an arm, leg, tail, etc. is shown in-frame
heavily simplified or greatly lacking detail
only appearing as a silhouette

Backgrounds with no discernable features (i.e. if it is only solid color, gradients, simple or template patterns, etc.) do not count towards rewards.

If you are reusing a background, you do not receive the bonuses on subsequent submissions.

For example

Examples of simple backgrounds include: only sky/hill/grass with little to no foliage or props or features.

Examples of complex backgrounds include: many props used, plenty of foliage, or features such as buildings, fences, etc.
